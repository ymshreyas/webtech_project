Stock Market Technical Analysis
SMA

Simple Moving Averages (SMAs) smooth price data in stock analysis, aiding trend identification and support/resistance level detection. Calculated over defined intervals, they inform traders for informed decisions, crucial in technical analysis strategies.
MACD

Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) in stock market analysis measures the convergence or divergence of two exponential moving averages, typically the 12-period and 26-period EMAs. It helps traders identify trend reversals, momentum shifts, and potential buy or sell signals, enhancing decision-making in technical analysis strategies.
Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands in stock market analysis consist of a moving average line with upper and lower bands that represent standard deviations from the average. They help traders gauge volatility, identify potential price reversal points, and determine overbought or oversold conditions, crucial for making informed trading decisions.


About Stock Market

The stock market is a platform where investors buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies. It serves as a barometer of economic health, reflecting investor sentiment and corporate performance, while offering opportunities for individuals and institutions to invest and potentially grow their wealth.
